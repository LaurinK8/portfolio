Personal Interests

Overview

I am passionate about building applications that are visually appealing, intuitive, and interactive. My strength lies in frontend development, where I enjoy designing how things look and feel, creating user-friendly and engaging interfaces. While I have experience in backend technologies like Node.js, my natural focus is on the visual and interactive side of web applications. I enjoy creative problem-solving, particularly when it comes to improving the user experience through thoughtful design and smooth interactions.

Most of my experience so far has been in web development, where I’ve worked with technologies such as Leaflet for interactive maps and Chart.js for data visualization. I often work with raw HTML, CSS, and JavaScript but am eager to deepen my knowledge of modern frameworks to build more dynamic and scalable solutions.

At the same time, I’ve enjoyed exploring mobile app development, such as my logging app for dormakaba and a current Maps App project at university. This has sparked my interest in expanding my skill set to native app development while continuing to strengthen my expertise in web technologies.

Looking ahead, I see myself as a full-stack developer with a strong frontend focus, combining design-driven thinking with the technical skills needed to deliver seamless, end-to-end digital experiences.
